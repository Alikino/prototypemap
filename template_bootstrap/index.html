TravelMap - find people to share your time with

For outdoor activities, traveling, parties, and more

Meet & party

SPONT is an app that will help you to find people on the map who want to spend time the same way as you do. Want to quickly find company for a party, to visit an interesting event or even just for a walk? Looking for friends while travelling? Just write about your plans right on the map and find people who want to join you.

Or you can join people to spend time in interesting and unusual ways. Open the map and see what people are doing anywhere in the world. You may find a great idea for a pastime!
